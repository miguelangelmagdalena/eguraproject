    Our Goal

    To achieve a network of Egura Cooperation Trials around the tropical and subtropical planet in open conventions within local universities and small pilot communities and villages next to coastal marine waters, or inland beside or above a polluted water body (rivers, lakes, lagoons, aquifers) where to deploy Egura Cooperation Plants to provide the partial needs of water and energy in order to help cope with some scarcity problems for families needing some push for their own challenge to get freedom and independence alone.

    Our Story

    In 1996 when an Egura like flash idea crossed our minds

    We were looking at the semidesert Araya Peninsula in Sucre, Venezuela; driving by the southern coast road around Cariaco Gulf and talking about how to get a greener Araya, a less thirsty people, a more independent society: we thought first into ‘injecting’ seawater to the Earth Cortex as the well known principle of ‘more meters down the ground more Celsius degrees in temperature, so we could boil seawater underground and recover steam on surface terrain to harvest pressure (energy) and re condense water vapor to get freshwater…
