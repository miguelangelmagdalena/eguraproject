Our History

How Egura Project was Born in our Minds

We were teenagers in 1996 when an Egura like flash idea crossed our minds looking at the semidesert Araya Peninsula in Sucre, Venezuela; my best friends Juan Carlos Raffensperger Lpez and Csar Augusto Dommar Valerio and I were driving by the southern coast road around Cariaco Gulf, with Araya there to the North, a ref strip of land, historical where Humboldt described Jaguars once, and where Dutch traded pearls, French buccaneers attacked, and Spain built a large Salt industry centuries ago.

We were talking about how to get a greener Araya, a less thirsty people, a more independent society: we thought first into ‘injecting’ seawater to the Earth Cortex as the well known principle of ‘more meters down the ground more Celsius degrees in temperature, so we could boil seawater underground and recover steam on surface terrain to harvest pressure (energy) and re condense water vapor to get freshwater… Maybe that could also work in future Egura Farms, certainly we will try it out too, but, there stayed that spark of neurons in 3 young brains, but 17 years later, in an adult part of my life, very apart, geographically from my beloved friends (one in Calgary, one still in Cuman, our home),

I was in the public library of Uharte, Navarre, my new hometown since I came here self-exiled, an ex-pat from totalitarian Venezuela, in 2013. That very year I started, invented the word ‘Egura’ from the 2 words of paleolithic living fossil language, the Basque, Euskera, the language of my father’s ancestors: Eguzki (Sun) + Ura (Water).

Our Mission

Become into a global virtuous enterprise for energy, water, and economic development of tropical and subtropical nations using our know-how in order to get open societies on the road to freedom.

We envision and project during, and beyond this century of hope, a time when the easy energy and easy water for all is available for free market and non-coercitive, with voluntary exchanges among free individual citizens, in liberty, thus constructing a social environment of a natural economy, with the invisible pulse of natural liberty, an inalienable natural law, and natural right, leading us all towards a virtuous spontaneous order of progress and growth with all respect for Nature and the active recognition of all human beings of planet Earth, the only and unique spaceship in the infinite and eternal Universe. The limit of our sacred Liberty is the sacred Liberty of our sisters and brothers of this vast human world. Let’s go, Liberty!

Our Vision

Objective General

To achieve a network of Egura Cooperation Trials around the tropical and subtropical planet in open conventions within local universities and small pilot communities and villages next to coastal marine waters, or inland beside or above a polluted water body (rivers, lakes, lagoons, aquifers) where to deploy Egura Cooperation Plants to provide the partial needs of water and energy (plus the rest of valuable byproducts: salts, plankton, O2, H2) in order to help cope with some scarcity problems for families needing some push for their own challenge to get freedom and independence alone. ii) to construct a network of Egura Business Sustainable Plants Companies within suitable nations like the US, Brazil, Venezuela, Chile, Saudi Arabia, Israel, Nigeria, Kuwait, UAE, Japan, Bahrein, South Africa, Qatar, Australia, Oman, Taiwan, South Korea, and Mediterranean and Black seas European nations (this latter in a sole Egura Europe Co.). This Egura Business Division is created to fund the Egura Cooperation Trials above!
